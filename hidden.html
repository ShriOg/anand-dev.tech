Future Ideas (Unfiltered)

Procedural Music Generator

Generate ambient music based on code activity. Typing triggers notes, errors create dissonance, successful builds resolve to major chords. Silly? Maybe. Fun? Definitely.

Git History Visualization

3D visualization of git history where commits are particles, branches are streams, and merges create collisions. Could be beautiful or a complete mess.

Keyboard Heatmap

Track which keys I press most while coding. Visualize over time. Might reveal interesting patterns about coding habits. Or just show that I press backspace too much.

AI Pair Programmer Voice

Instead of chat-based AI assistance, have a voice that speaks suggestions out loud. Like having a pair programmer but without the social awkwardness.

Code Archaeology Tool

Tool that traces the history of a specific line of code. Who wrote it, when, why (from commit messages), and how many times it's been modified. Understand code through its history.
