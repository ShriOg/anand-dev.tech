About Me

I'm a developer who believes in building software that's both functional and thoughtfully designed. My focus is on creating clean, purposeful solutions that solve real problems.

How I Think

I approach problems by first understanding the core need, then working backwards to find the simplest solution. I value clarity over cleverness, and I believe that well-structured code is easier to maintain and scale. Every project is an opportunity to learn something new and refine my craft.
